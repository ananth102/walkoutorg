We support the right to peceful assemble and we intend to help people do just that.

How we can help

We have created a user interface that makes it easy to organize your own events easily and you can browse other events happening near you. We integrated mapping algorithms to assist you in finding the shortest route to your event.

Features

Our website can assist you in creating new walkouts/protests and it also implements the same mapping algorithms that we use in our mobile app. It also displays the number of people are going to attend and if the necessary permissions have been granted for the protest to occur. Our mobile app contains all of these features and compliments our website perfectly.
